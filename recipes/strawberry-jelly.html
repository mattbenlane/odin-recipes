strawberry-jelly

Information

British strawberry jelly is a fruit-flavored gelatin dessert, similar to what is known in the United States as Jell-O. Not a jam or fruit spread as the term "jelly" might indicate in the States, British jelly is a beautiful, fruit-forward, and refreshing dessert that can take elaborate forms and be decorated with intricate fruit arrangements.
